About Us

Newolf Society is a Haven for thinkers, hackers and builders.
We primarily focus on networking, learning and building super cool projects together!
Some of our important features are:-


  • Learning Latest Tech

    Peer to Peer Learning, Regular Discussions and webinars on latest tech.
    Personalized learning tracks are available for you to choose from and Industry Talks for crazy exposure!


  • Perfect Platform to Network

    Network with like minded folks, Dedicated Channels for Different Tech, Build Teams for Projects, Startups and more!
    This community is run completely virtually(through discord) for members to interact with each other.


  • Working on Good Projects

    Regular updates on Hackathons and Competitions, Resource Recommendations for your projects, Tech Support on Request.

About the Icebreaker Program


  • To get in the community, we have an icebreaker, a 2 week program built for you to network, learn and build with your teammates
  • We have a lot of fun interactive sessions and amazing beginner webinars to keep you awake and interactive!
  • On the final day, you would be showcasing your projects to all of us.
  • If you successfully finish the icebreaker, you are welcome into the community.
  • Becoming a member is free of cost. We do not take any money. You just have to be dedicated to learn, build and finish your project.
